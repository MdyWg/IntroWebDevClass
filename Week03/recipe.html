Tiramisu Recipe

    A recipe for making tiramisu, a delicious dessert, in just four steps

    Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream (360 ml)
    • Cocoa powder
    • 8 ounce container room temperature mascarpone cheese (225 g)
    • 1/3 cup granulated sugar (67 g)
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (5 ml)
    • 1 1/2 cups cold espresso (360 ml)
    • 3 Tablespoons coffee flavored liqueur (optional) (45 ml)
    • 1 package Lady Fingers

    Instructions

    1. Add whipping cream to a mixing bowl and beat on medium speed with electric mixers (or use a stand mixer). Slowly add sugar and vanilla and continue to beat until stiff peaks. Add mascarpone cheese and fold in until combined. Set aside.
    2. Add coffee and liqueur to a shallow bowl. Dip the lady fingers in the coffee and lay them in a single layer on the bottom of an 8Ã—8'' or similar size pan.
    3. Smooth half of the mascarpone mixture over the top. Add another layer of dipped lady fingers. Smooth remaining mascarpone cream over the top.
    4. Dust cocoa powder generously over the top. Refrigerate for at least 3-4 hours or up to overnight before serving.
